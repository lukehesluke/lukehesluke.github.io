Euclidean Construction

Euclidean Construction is a method for constructing figures on a canvas with just two tools: a straightedge and a compass.

Two points

You start with two points [1]:

From this starting point, you can construct anything [2].

Circles

Place the compass with one end on one point and the other end on another point and you can draw a circle.

Lines

Use the straightedge to draw a line through two points.

Or a line segment between the two points.
